## General

- `/discaching help` - Open help menu.
- `/discaching profile` - View your stats, stickers and achievements.
- `/discaching inventory` - Browse your inventory.
- `/discaching balance` - View your current G\$ balance.
- `/discaching daily` - Claim your daily reward (G\$25).
- `/discaching quests` - View your daily quests and claim quest rewards.
- `/discaching shop` - Open shop and buy/sell items.
- `/discaching leaderboard` - View top players by category.
- `/discaching gift [user] [amount]` - Send G\$ to another player.
- `/discaching open [Vote Crate|Quest Crate] [amount]` - Open crates.
- `/discaching credits` - Project credits.

## Caching Gameplay

- `/discaching hide` - Start or continue hiding a cache.
- `/discaching find` - Log found/DNF/note on a cache.
- `/discaching nearby` - Show 5 nearest unfound caches.
- `/discaching cache_info [cache_id]` - Detailed cache info.
- `/discaching cache_finds [cache_id]` - View cache logs.
- `/discaching locations [Rural|City]` - List locations and descriptions.

## Travel and Position

- `/discaching travel [cache_id]` - Travel to a cache, or open the map to navigate.
- `/discaching travel cancel_current:true` - Cancel active travel.
- `/discaching location` - Current coordinates and travel status.
- `/discaching map` - Map with your location and published caches.
- `/discaching distance_to_cache [cache_id]` - Distance and time estimate to a cache.

## Trackables

- `/discaching trackable_activate [private_code]` - Activate a trackable you own.
- `/discaching trackable_view [public_code]` - View info for a trackable.
- `/discaching trackable_grab [public_code]` - Grab a trackable from a cache.
- `/discaching trackable_discover [private_code] [log]` - Discover a shared trackable.
