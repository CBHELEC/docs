### **<u>What is Discaching?</u>**

Inspired by what all of you love, the developer(s) of Tracker have created a one-of-a-kind virtual caching sandbox, where you have free will to hide, find, and travel, to your heart's content.\
All inside of Discord, you can enjoy caching without needing real equipment, or without needing to worry about weather.

## Quick Facts

- New players start with **G\$100**.
- Daily claim gives **G\$25**.
- You must be within about **0.1 km (100m)** of a cache to log a find.
- Hides need to be submitted and approved before they are published.

Feel free to discover any trackable private codes you see on here!
