**<u>What is Discaching?</u>**\
Inspired by what all of you love, the developer(s) of Tracker have created a one-of-a-kind virtual caching sandbox, where you have free will to hide, find, and travel, to your heart's content.

**<u>How do I get started?</u>**\
To get started, try and find a cache. We have placed GX1 at your location (virtually) for you to find in order to get a taste of how Discaching works.
