## Daily Quests

Use `/discaching quests`.

How it works:

- You get 4 quests per day.
- Typical mix: 2 easy, 1 medium, 1 hard.
- Completing quests gives claimable **Quest Crates**.
- Claiming is manual via button in quests command.

Quest types include:

- Finding
- Travelling
- Spending
- Claiming Daily
- Giving Favourite Points
- Gifting
- Trackable stuff
- Shop purchasing
- Hiding
- Writing logs
- Writing instrument usage
- Vehicle usage
- Location visits
- Inventory item count
- FTF
- Sticker placing/collecting

## Collectible Stickers

- Buy from shop.
- Place into caches.
- Collect from caches.

## Achievement Stickers

Awarded on milestones for categories like:

- Finds
- Hides
- FTF
- Total spending
- Balance
- Favorite points received
- Trackables owned/activated/discovered
- Pencil breaks

## Profile and Leaderboards

`/discaching profile` is for personal stats and achievement / sticker collections. \
`/discaching leaderboard` is to see rankings for stats such as:

- Money
- Finds
- Hides
- Favorite points
- Trackables
- Votes
