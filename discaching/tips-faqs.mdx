## Practical Tips

1. Buy writing instruments early; <Tooltip tip="Unless a cache has a writing instrument inside - this is added by the owner during hiding." headline="no writing instrument" cta="Read our Hiding guide" href="/discaching/hide-find">no writing instrument</Tooltip>means no Found It logs.
2. Keep at least one short-range and one long-range transport option.
3. Use `/discaching nearby` before committing travel.
4. Save hide drafts if you are still deciding on final location.
6. Use medium/large caches if you want more swag/sticker interactions.

## FAQ

### Why can I not log this cache as found?

Common reasons:

- You are too far away.
- Cache is unpublished.
- It is your own cache.
- You have no usable writing instrument.

### Why can I not start another trip?

You likely already have active travel. Use `/discaching location` to check status, or `/discaching travel cancel_current:true` to cancel your current travel.

### Why did my trip only go partway?

Your selected vehicle/ticket did not have enough max distance remaining for full route.

### Why can I not exchange swag in this cache?

Swag/sticker exchange is limited to larger cache sizes (M/L/XL).

### How do I get more G\$ quickly?

- Claim daily.
- Complete daily quests.
- Sell unused items.
- Open earned crates.
- Find easter eggs.
- Report unfair glitches.
